🌌 Orbit Launch

Launch rockets from Earth into orbit! Each rocket flies to a random distance. The further it goes, the bigger your win.

HOW TO PLAY

1. Choose volatility level (Low / Med / High / Degen)
2. Set your bet per rocket
3. Spam LAUNCH (click or Space) — each click = 1 rocket = 1 bet
4. Watch rockets fly outward through orbit zones
5. Each rocket auto-pays based on which zone it reaches

ORBIT ZONES

Rockets fly from Earth outward through concentric orbit zones. Each zone has a fixed multiplier. The further your rocket reaches, the higher the payout. Most rockets explode near Earth (0x loss). Rare rockets reach Deep Space for massive wins.

VOLATILITY

Med — Balanced risk and reward
High — Most rockets explode, survivors pay big
Degen — Almost all explode, rare mega wins up to 2500×
